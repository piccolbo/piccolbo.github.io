The language R is in a phase of tremendous growth and reaching the mainstream. Like others, I am working on expanding its capability and I see many years of success ahead. But the search for what is next has already started and it's not too early: R appeared first in 1993, some 20 years ago. It takes a long time to take a language from conception to success.
